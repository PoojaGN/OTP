Superman is a comic book superhero who appears in comic books by DC Comics. He is widely considered to be an American cultural icon. Created by writer Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster in 1932 while both were living in Cleveland, Ohio, and sold to Detective Comics, Inc. (later DC Comics) in 1938, Superman has appeared in various radio serials, television programs, films, newspaper strips, and video games.
